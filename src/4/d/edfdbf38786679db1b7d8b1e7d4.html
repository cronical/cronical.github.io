Narrative

 

MARRIAGE RECORD

Clark County, Arkansas Book A page 151-125A & 152-126A

State of Arkansas,
County of Clark.

I do hereby certify that the rites of matrimony were duly and legally
celebrated between Samuel C. Thornton and Amanda Franklin on the 12th day
of February A.D. 1837 by me.

JOHN H. NELSON, J.P.

State of Arkansas,
County of Clark.

I, Jas. S. Ward, Deputy Clerk of the Clark Circuit Court & Exofficio
recorder for said county, do hereby certify that the above and foregoing
Certificate of Marriage was this day produced to me and ordered to be
Recorded which is duly done. Witness my hand as deputy clerk & Exofficio
Recorder for said County this 29th day of March A.D. 1837.

JAS. S. WARD, Dep. Clk.
& Exofficio Recorder.
