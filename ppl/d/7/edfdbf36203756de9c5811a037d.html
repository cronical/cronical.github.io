        MR. WILLIAM BROOKS (from Obituaries & Death Notices of Pope Co, AR)

         

         

        Mr. William Brooks, died at his home, four miles from town, on Friday, March 24, 1883 Mr. Brooks was a bout sixty years of age, and had been a citizen of this county and community about forty years. He was born and reared in North Carolina, and came from that state to this county about forty years ago. He had been a member in good standing of the Cumberland Presbyterian Church about forty years. He had been married twice and was the father of eleven children, three of whom are dead. He leaves a wife and eight children to mourn his absence. Mr. Brooks was an industrious man, a good citizen, and a leading man in his community. He was a devoted husband and a kind father. He died in the triumphs of a living faith and is gone to his reward. To the bereaved we tender our heartfelt sympathies and urge them to trust the same God that he trusted.
