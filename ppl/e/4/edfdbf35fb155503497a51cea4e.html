        I am researching the DAVIS name in Surry Co.

        Leonard DAVIS, b. c 1751 in Orange Co., VA.
        He married Susannah BURRIS in VA. In 1793, he owned land on Stewart's Creek in Surry Co.
        In 1808, his father, Mathew DAVIS, who was living in Surry Co., left him everything except the land he(Mathew) owned.
        In 1809, Leonard moved to Overton Co. TN.
